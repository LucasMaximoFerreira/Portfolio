About me

Who am I?

Hi, my name is Lucas Máximo and currently I work as a Data Engineer.

Passionate about technology and always looking for new learning. Currently, I'm a Data Engineer, working with Google Cloud Platform tools, with ETL's methods, Pipelines, using their tools such as Data Fusion, Data Studio and several tools for data manipulation, in addition to SQL queries, in the BigData of the work platform with NoSQL, for queries of LandSat/MODIS and Sentinel satellite images. Currently, I participate in FIRST, a robotics group with the aim of building and programming robots of industrial proportions in order to compete in the FRC - First Robotics Competition that in 2020 was held in the city of Orlando.

Telling a little more about me, let's say that most of the time I’m studying, in addition to the different things I learn every day, I have advanced English and basic in French, I’m a nice and friendly person and I love helping people in any kind of problem, I can say that I am a musician, having experience in drums, guitar and piano and what I like to do most is traveling.
